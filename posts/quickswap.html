Quick Swap ADD-ON

NEVER DIE AGAIN!

About The Addon

Quick Swap is a survival utility addon that protects you in dangerous situations. When your health drops to 3 hearts or below, the addon automatically searches your inventory for a Totem of Undying or a Shield and equips it to your offhand instantly.

Add-ON Features

- Automatic Swap: Equips items to offhand when health is low (3 Hearts).

- Custom Menu: Type !swap to select your preferred panic item.

- Priority System: Choose whether to prioritize Totems or Shields.

- Intelligent Search: Only swaps if the offhand is empty or holding a different item.

How To Use

Activation

Once the behavior pack is active, the system is automatic. Simply carry Totems or Shields in your inventory.

Open Preference Menu

Type !swap in the game chat to open the configuration menu. This will not be visible to other players.

Settings Menu

In the menu, you can select which item the script should look for first. If your preferred item is missing, it will automatically look for the alternative.

Make sure you have "Beta APIs" enabled in your world experiments for the script to work!
