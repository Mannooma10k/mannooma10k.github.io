Pocket Smelter ADD-ON

AN ENCHANTED FURNACE IN YOUR POCKET.

About The Addon

Smelt on the go! The Pocket Smelter cooks food & ores 2x faster, Toggle fuel modes:
burn your XP or use Coal/Logs from your inventory,
Supports full stack batch cooking. No placing blocks required—just hold and use!

Add-ON Features

- Process entire stacks of items instantly with a single click.

- Cooks food twice as fast.

- Toggle between burning your own XP or using inventory coal.

- Smelt items instantly anywhere without ever placing a furnace block.

How To Use

Craft Pocket Smelter Item

To make the Pocket Smelter Item,
You need 8x cobblestones and 1x campfire

Main menu

After that hold the item and Right Click or use aim button in mobile, to open main menu.

Smeltable items are displayed here once discovered or added to your inventory
Smelt these items using XP. Processing takes 3 seconds per stack, regardless of quantity.

Pocket Smelter Modes

Low on XP? Switch modes to use regular coal or wood instead.
Just keep them in your inventory, and Pocket Smelter will automatically consume these resources to stay powered.
