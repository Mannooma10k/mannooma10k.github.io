Void Satchel ADD-ON

KEEP YOUR INVENTORY CLEAN.

About The Addon

An automated trash system that filters selected items
,saves inventory space, and features a 1,000-durability repair system,
allowing you to focus on collecting rare ores only.

Add-ON Features

- Program five different block types for automatic deletion.

- Easily configure up to five different block types.

- Automatically deletes junk blocks to save inventory space.

- Fix your broken satchel using pearls and experience.

How To Use

Craft Void Satchel Item

To make the Void Satchel Item,
You need 2x obsidian, 2x ender_pearls and 1x chest

Main menu

After that hold the item and Shift + Right Click or use aim button in mobile, this form will pop out.

Add blocks

You can clear the slot or choose the block/item you want to remove

You can add to 5 blocks/items in Void Satchel
You Can turn Void Satchel on or off through the menu or right click while holding the item

Void Satchel Item

Before Activating Void Satchel

After Activating Void Satchel

The Void Satchel Can clear 1000 blocks/items off your inventory after that it will be broke
In order to repair it you will need 2 ender_pearls to fully
repair it through the anvil

3. GO TO WORLD SETTINGS > EXPERIMENTS.
4. YOU MUST ENABLE "BETA APIS"!
